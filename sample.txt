Donkey ki Duniya

Ek gaon mein ek donkey rehta tha, jiska naam tha Chintu. Chintu ek bahut hi pyara donkey tha, jo roz subah gaon ke kheton mein jaakar kaam karta tha. Gaon ke sab log us donkey ko bahut pasand karte the.

Chintu ko apni zindagi bahut achi lagti thi, lekin ek din usne socha, "Main sirf ek donkey hi nahi, main kuch bada kar sakta hoon!" Toh usne decide kiya ki woh gaon ke bahar jaake naye dost banayega.

Raste mein usne ek aur donkey dekha, jo thoda udaas lag raha tha. Chintu ne us donkey se kaha, "Chal mere saath, hum dono milke naye adventures karenge!"

Dono donkey milkar naye gaon gaye, jahan unhone bahut saare naye donkey dost banaye. Har donkey ki apni kahani thi, par sab ek dusre ki madad karte the.

Ek din, jab gaon mein barish hui, toh sab donkey apni chhatri leke ek jagah jama ho gaye. Sabne milke decide kiya ki woh ek donkey festival manayenge, jisme sab apni kahaniyan batayenge.

Woh din aaya, aur sab donkey ne apni life ke mazedaar kisse sunaye. Chintu bhi apni dosti aur adventure ki kahani sabko sunakar sabka dil jeet gaya.

Tab se, gaon mein har saal donkey festival hota hai, jisme sab donkey milke khushiyan baant-te hain, aur dosti aur mazakiya kahaniyan sunate hain.
